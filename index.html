FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

I’m unsure about the place. How can I chat with the host before booking?

You need to create an account in order to chat with the host. If you've logged in, click on the booking page, then chat with the host.

How will I receive confirmation of my booking?

You will receive an invoice after payment. You can also check your invoice on profile.

How to post photos or videos on ThrillX?

You need to create an account first before posting anything.

Why can't I join a community?

You need to create an account in order to join a community. Some communities are private and some are public. You need to send a request to join the private one.
